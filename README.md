# 🎓 AI Student Companion — Voice AI for Distraction-Free Learning

voice-first AI tutor built on ESP32-S3 that lets 
students ask academic questions hands-free — without picking up their phone.

## The Problem
Students reach for their phone to look up answers.
One search leads to social media. 30 minutes lost.

## The Solution
A dedicated AI voice device that sits on your desk.
Ask a question → Get an instant voice answer → Keep studying.
No screen. No distraction. No compromise.

## What It Does
- 🎤 Push-to-talk or toggle mode
- 🔊 Native AI voice
- 🧠 Remembers what you taught it — saves to Google Sheets
- 📖 Recalls saved notes by subject and chapter
- 🌐 Real-time answers via Google Search grounding
- 🔁 Replay last answer without re-asking
- ⚙️ Configure everything from your phone — no coding after flash
- 💾 Google Sheets as a database — edit from mobile anytime
